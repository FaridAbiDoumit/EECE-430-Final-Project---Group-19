# Standard Operating Procedures

## Core Rules
1. Do not lose data or silently discard user information.
2. Keep the codebase and directory structure clean, minimal, and professional.
3. Maintain good git hygiene: small focused changes, clear commits, and no unnecessary generated files in version control.
4. Prefer refactoring toward simpler, less bloated designs when it improves clarity and maintainability.
5. Use best practices where they materially improve correctness, structure, or maintainability.
6. Keep comments minimal. No comments are preferred over low-value comments.
7. Record main decisions in `log/`.

## Repository Hygiene
- Keep top-level directories intentional and few.
- Avoid duplicate files, dead scaffolding, and unused modules.
- Do not introduce dependencies unless they are needed.
- Keep naming consistent across apps, templates, and routes.
